Frequently Asked Questions

Are these fabrics officially approved by DAV Institutions?
Yes. All fabrics are developed strictly as per DAV's approved shades, weaves, and standards for the 2026-27 uniform redesign.

What types of fabrics are available?
Full range of shirting and suiting - plain, checks, stripes, and blended cotton/poly-cotton options.

How can I become a preferred partner or authorised supplier?
Connect with our regional manager or fill the Partner Enrollment Form. You'll receive catalog access and support details.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
Standard MOQ is 300 metres per shade, with flexible options for approved DAV partners.

Do you maintain ready stock?
Yes. Ready stock for fast-moving shades and make-to-order options for bulk requirements.

What is the delivery timeline?
Ready stock dispatch in 24 hours; make-to-order within 7-10 working days.

How is quality assured?
Each batch passes 18+ lab-tested parameters including colour fastness, shrinkage, tear strength, GSM accuracy & Finish stability.

Can I get samples or shade cards before ordering?
Yes. Free swatches and certified shade cards available on request.

What kind of partner support does Sparsh Fab provide?
Preferred partners get priority dispatch, marketing kits, school presentation support, and access to the Legacy Circle loyalty program a platform to grow "Do Kadam Aage."

Are pricing and payment terms flexible?
Yes. Customised pricing and credit terms based on order volume and partnership level.
